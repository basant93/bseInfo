The Project

Riverwalk Outlet Collections is a landmark project offering a sophisticated shopping and dining marketplace destination for locals, tourists and convention attendees, offering a connection to both the history and future of the city of New Orleans with its prominent location adjacent to the Mississippi River.

Providing over 250,000 square feet of retail, dining and entertainment venues, exterior terraces and interior walkways offer commanding views of the river and provide unique interactions with the working docks of the Mississippi. Retail spaces include outdoor market venues and dining on Spanish Plaza; stylish shops on three levels and a terrace-level food hall specializing in regional, local and international foods.

City Connections

The riverfront location is a unique asset to the project and provides a foundation to draw upon its cultural inspiration. The project infuses the vitality and dynamic vibrancy of the.

Originally donated to the City of New Orleans by the Spanish Government, Spanish Plaza provides the city link via an open courtyard between the Mississippi and two major downtown streets, Canal and Poydras. This area provides an urban environment of alfresco dining, street vendors and retail shops that face the plaza providing access to the main entry into the Street Promenade interior district as well as the Hilton Hotel, adjacent wharfs, cruise ships, ferry terminals and the adjacent aquarium.

Stores along the water have the opportunity to merchandise along the wharf where people stroll, sit and eat reinforcing the riverfront and wharf experience.

Prominent entry portals located at strategic points from the city set the tone for the architecture of the project with their modern design sensibility. Dramatic, over-scaled sign letters parading across the building on the east river side, are easily identified by arriving cruise ships. Featuring contemporary design, the building exterior provides a stimulating and transparent view into the interior upper level terrace generating excitement about the destination center.

The spirit of Riverwalk is created by its crisp, contemporary building design and interiors. The imaginative use of transparency, color, lighting and graphics allow a select group of Tenants the opportunity to feature a variety of merchandise selections, contributing to this spirit, while creating an authentic, vibrant and memorable regional retail destination.
